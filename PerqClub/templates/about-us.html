Our Story

Creating a Culture, Not Just Coffee

PERQ Club was born from a simple observation: young people in India love café culture but find regular visits financially challenging. Meanwhile, cafés struggle with inconsistent footfall, especially during non-peak hours.

Our founder, xyz, passionate about youth brands and community experiences, envisioned a solution that benefits both sides - a membership model that makes café visits more affordable while helping cafés increase their traffic.

Today, PERQ Club is growing into a cultural movement that connects like-minded individuals through shared experiences in India's vibrant café scene.

"We're not just offering coffee. We're creating a cultural movement that makes café experiences accessible to all."

The Problem

  • Young people love café culture but avoid going regularly due to high prices

  • Cafés struggle with low footfall during non-peak hours

  • No community-based membership model in India that helps both sides win

RedSeer reports that Gen Z in India is highly experience-focused and willing to spend on lifestyle if there's value or exclusivity attached.

Our Solution

  • PERQ Club membership gives free coffee and exclusive discounts at partner cafés

  • Members save money while enjoying their favorite café experiences

  • Partner cafés increase traffic, especially during low hours

  • Build a community of café enthusiasts across India
